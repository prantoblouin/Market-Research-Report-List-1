<p><p>The intravenous (IV) port market is categorized into two main types: fixed base and mobile base. Fixed base IV ports are designed to be permanently affixed in a specific location, providing stable access for ongoing treatments. They are ideal for long-term therapies. In contrast, mobile base IV ports are portable and adaptable, allowing for flexibility in various clinical settings. This mobility makes them suitable for patients requiring treatment in different locations or those with varying medical needs during their care.</p></p>
