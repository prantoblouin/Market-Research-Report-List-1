<p><p>Electronic Article Surveillance (EAS) labels are categorized mainly into Acousto-Magnetic (AM), Microwave EAS, Electro-Magnetic EAS, UHF, and Gen 2 RFID EAS. AM labels use magnetic fields for detection, while Microwave EAS relies on microwave signals. Electro-Magnetic EAS operates through electromagnetic fields. UHF systems leverage ultra-high frequency for better range and speed. Gen 2 RFID EAS combines radio-frequency identification technology with anti-theft features, providing enhanced inventory tracking and security solutions across various retail environments.</p></p>
