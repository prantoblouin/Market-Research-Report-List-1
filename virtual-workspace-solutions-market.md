<p><p>Virtual workspace solutions are categorized into three main types: public, private, and hybrid markets. Public solutions are hosted on shared infrastructure, accessible to multiple organizations, promoting cost-effectiveness and scalability. Private solutions offer dedicated resources for a single organization, ensuring enhanced security and control. Hybrid markets combine elements of both, allowing businesses to leverage the benefits of public resources while maintaining sensitive data on private infrastructure. This flexibility caters to diverse needs, balancing performance, security, and cost considerations.</p></p>
