<p><p>The companion animal external dewormer market is segmented into injections and unguents. Injection-based dewormers provide systemic treatment, delivering active ingredients directly into the bloodstream for rapid effectiveness against parasites. They are often favored for their precision and long-lasting effects. Unguents, on the other hand, are topical treatments applied to the skin, offering ease of use and convenience. They create a barrier against external parasites and are typically favored for their non-invasive application and ability to treat localized infestations effectively.</p></p>
