In contrast, polycrystalline ceramics consist of multiple crystal grains, offering versatility and cost-effectiveness for various industrial uses. Each type serves distinct needs in fields such as aerospace, automotive, and manufacturing, where durability and performance are critical for tools and components.</p></p>
