<p><strong>The Laser Engraver Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CO2 Laser Engraver</li><li>Fiber Laser Engraver</li><li>Diode Laser Engraver</li><li>Nd:YAG Laser Engraver</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The laser engraver market comprises four main types: </p><p>1. **CO2 Laser Engravers** utilize gas lasers, ideal for non-metal materials like wood and acrylic.</p><p>2. **Fiber Laser Engravers** employ solid-state technology, perfect for metal engraving and offering high precision and speed.</p><p>3. **Diode Laser Engravers** are compact and cost-effective, suitable for hobbyists and light-duty applications on various materials.</p><p>4. **Nd:YAG Laser Engravers** use solid-state lasers, effective for marking metals and creating deep engravings, often found in industrial settings.</p></p>
