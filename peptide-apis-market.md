<p><p>The peptide APIs market is primarily segmented into Liquid Phase Peptide Synthesis (LPPS) and Solid Phase Peptide Synthesis (SPPS). LPPS involves synthesizing peptides in a solution, allowing for longer sequences and complex modifications, making it suitable for certain therapeutic applications. In contrast, SPPS utilizes solid supports to facilitate the sequential addition of amino acids, offering high efficiency and faster synthesis for shorter peptides. Both techniques serve crucial roles in drug development and biopharmaceutical production, catering to varied peptide requirements.</p></p>
