<p><strong>The Two-Way Valve Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Full Bore</li><li>Reduced Bore</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The two-way valve market is categorized into full bore and reduced bore types, each serving distinct applications. Full bore valves feature a wide opening, allowing for maximum flow and minimal pressure loss, making them ideal for high-capacity systems. In contrast, reduced bore valves have a smaller opening, which can create higher pressure drops but are often more compact and cost-effective for lower flow applications. Both types are essential in various industries, including oil and gas, water treatment, and manufacturing.</p></p>
