<p><strong>The Air Tightness Leak Detector Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Differential Pressure Type</li><li>Direct Pressure Type</li><li>Flow Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Air Tightness Leak Detector market includes three main types: Differential Pressure Type, Direct Pressure Type, and Flow Type. Differential Pressure Type detectors measure pressure differences to identify leaks in structures, providing high sensitivity. Direct Pressure Type detectors assess the pressure within a sealed environment directly, suitable for quick assessments. Flow Type detectors measure air flow rates to identify leaks by observing discrepancies in expected flow versus actual flow, making them effective for larger systems. Each type serves specific applications and accuracy needs.</p></p>
