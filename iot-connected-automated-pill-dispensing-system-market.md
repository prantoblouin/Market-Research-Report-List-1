<p><p>The IoT-connected Automated Pill Dispensing System market comprises two main types: Centralized and Decentralized Automated Dispensing Systems. Centralized systems store and dispense medications from a central location, often used in large healthcare facilities to enhance efficiency and accuracy. In contrast, Decentralized systems are deployed closer to the point of care, such as in patient homes or small clinics, allowing for personalized medication management. Both systems leverage IoT technology to monitor dosages, enhance adherence, and streamline inventory management.</p></p>
