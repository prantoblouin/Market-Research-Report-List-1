<p><p>The foil capacitor market consists of three primary types: metal film, ceramic film, and polymer film capacitors. Metal film capacitors are known for their low distortion and precision, making them ideal for audio applications. Ceramic film capacitors offer high stability and reliability, commonly used in high-frequency circuits. Polymer film capacitors feature low equivalent series resistance (ESR) and high capacitance values, suitable for power applications. Each type serves distinct applications, contributing to diverse electronic designs and performance requirements.</p></p>
