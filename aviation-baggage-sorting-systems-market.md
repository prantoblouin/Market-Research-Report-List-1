<p><p>Aviation baggage sorting systems play a crucial role in airport operations by efficiently managing luggage transport. Two major market types are tilt tray sorters and cross belt sorters. Tilt tray sorters utilize hinged trays that tilt to redirect baggage to various destinations, optimizing space and flow. In contrast, cross belt sorters employ individual belts that move bags to their respective chutes, ensuring quick and accurate sorting. Both systems enhance operational efficiency, reduce delays, and improve overall passenger experience in handling baggage.</p></p>
