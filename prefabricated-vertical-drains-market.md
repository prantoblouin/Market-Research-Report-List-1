<p><p>The prefabricated vertical drains market features various design types, primarily wavy and fishbone shapes, each serving unique drainage needs. Wavy drains have undulating profiles that enhance water flow and soil consolidation, making them suitable for a wider range of soil conditions. In contrast, fishbone-shaped drains consist of a central vertical component with lateral branches, effectively distributing drainage over a broader area. Both types improve construction efficiency and reduce settlement time, catering to diverse applications in geotechnical engineering and infrastructure projects.</p></p>
