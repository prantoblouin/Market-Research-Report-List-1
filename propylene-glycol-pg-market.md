<p><p>Propylene Glycol (PG) is a versatile compound with three main market types. Industrial Grade PG is used in applications like antifreeze, de-icing agents, and hydraulic fluids due to its stability and low toxicity. Food Grade PG is utilized in food processing for moisture retention, flavoring, and as a solvent in food additives, ensuring safety for consumption. Pharmaceutical Grade PG is employed in the formulation of medicines and cosmetics, adhering to strict purity standards for safe human use.</p></p>
