<p><p>The Polymer Dispersed Liquid Crystal (PDLC) Smart Film market is divided into two primary types: None-Adhesive PDLC Smart Film and Self-Adhesive PDLC Smart Film. None-Adhesive PDLC Smart Film is designed to be applied using other adhesives or bonding techniques, requiring additional materials for installation. In contrast, Self-Adhesive PDLC Smart Film comes with a built-in adhesive backing, simplifying installation by allowing direct application to surfaces without the need for extra adhesives. Both types offer unique advantages for various applications in architecture and interior design.</p></p>
