<p><p>The Vertical Launch Anti-Submarine Missile (VLA) market is divided into two main types based on their propulsion systems: two-stage and three-stage missiles. Two-stage VLAs utilize an initial booster phase followed by a sustainer phase for extended range and speed, making them effective for quick targets. Three-stage VLAs add an additional propulsion stage, enhancing agility and range, thus enabling longer-distance engagements and improved accuracy against submerged threats. Both types cater to evolving naval warfare requirements.</p></p>
