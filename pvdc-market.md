<p><p>PVDC (Polyvinylidene Chloride) market can be divided into two primary types: PVDC resins and PVDC latex. PVDC resins are solid materials primarily used for packaging applications due to their excellent barrier properties against moisture and gases, enhancing shelf-life for perishable goods. In contrast, PVDC latex refers to a water-based dispersion used in coatings and adhesives, providing similar barrier characteristics while offering flexibility and ease of application. Both types cater to diverse industries, including food packaging, pharmaceuticals, and construction.</p></p>
