<p><strong>The Chemiluminescence Reagent Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Indirect Chemiluminescence</li><li>Direct Chemiluminescence</li><li>Electrochemiluminescence</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The chemiluminescence reagent market encompasses three primary types: indirect chemiluminescence, direct chemiluminescence, and electrochemiluminescence. Indirect chemiluminescence involves a secondary reaction where a luminescent signal is produced through the interaction of a substrate and an enzyme, commonly used in assays. Direct chemiluminescence generates light through the direct chemical reaction of luminescent compounds without additional components. Electrochemiluminescence combines electrochemical reactions with luminescence, producing measurable signals upon applying an electric current, enhancing detection sensitivity in various applications.</p></p>
