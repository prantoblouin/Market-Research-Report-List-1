<p><strong>The Mountain Bike Suspension Spring Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Air Spring</li><li>Coil Spring</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The mountain bike suspension spring market primarily features two types: air springs and coil springs. Air springs use compressed air to adjust the suspension's firmness, offering lightweight performance and easy customization for riders' preferences. They are favored for their ability to absorb small bumps effectively. In contrast, coil springs are made of metal coils, providing consistent performance and better stability during aggressive riding. They are typically more durable but heavier than air springs, appealing to riders seeking reliability and strength on challenging terrains.</p></p>
