<p><ul><li>Mechanical Fastening</li><li>Adhesive Bonding</li><li>Welding</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The plastic pipe jointing and welding market encompasses three primary types: mechanical fastening, adhesive bonding, and welding. Mechanical fastening involves connecting pipes using clamps or other fixtures for easy disassembly. Adhesive bonding uses specialized adhesives to create a strong, permanent seal between pipe surfaces, effective for various applications. Welding, including processes like heat fusion, involves melting pipe edges to form a robust joint, ensuring leak-proof connections. Each method caters to different installation requirements and material compatibility, facilitating diverse industrial applications.</p></p>
