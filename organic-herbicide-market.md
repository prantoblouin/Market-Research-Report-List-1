<p><p>The organic herbicide market comprises two main types: selective and non-selective herbicides. Selective herbicides target specific weeds while leaving desirable plants unharmed, making them ideal for agricultural settings where certain crops need protection. In contrast, non-selective herbicides eliminate all vegetation in the treated area, which can be useful for controlling overgrowth and preparing land. As demand for eco-friendly solutions grows, both types of organic herbicides are gaining popularity among environmentally conscious consumers and farmers.</p></p>
