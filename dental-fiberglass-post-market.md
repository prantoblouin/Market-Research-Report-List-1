<p><strong>The Dental Fiberglass Post Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Tapered</li><li>Parallel</li><li>Combination</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dental fiberglass post market is categorized into three main types: tapered, parallel, and combination posts. Tapered posts have a conical design that aids in easy placement and enhances retention within the root canal, making them suitable for smaller or curved canals. Parallel posts have a uniform diameter, providing consistent support and stability for larger restorations. Combination posts feature both tapered and parallel designs, allowing for adaptability in various clinical situations, optimizing retention and strength for diverse tooth structures.</p></p>
