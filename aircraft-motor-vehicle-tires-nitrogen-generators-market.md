<p><p>The Aircraft and Motor Vehicle Tires Nitrogen Generators market includes three main types: Pressure Swing Adsorption (PSA), Membrane, and Cryogenic Air. PSA utilizes the principle of selective adsorption to separate nitrogen from air under pressure, offering high purity and efficiency. Membrane technology relies on selective permeability, producing nitrogen by allowing specific gases to pass through membranes. Cryogenic Air separation involves cooling air to separate its components, yielding high purity nitrogen suitable for aviation and automotive applications, ensuring optimal tire performance and safety.</p></p>
