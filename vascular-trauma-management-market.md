<p><p>Vascular trauma management encompasses various surgical techniques to repair damaged blood vessels. Lateral arteriorrhaphy/venorrhaphy involves direct suturing of arteries or veins, while patch angioplasty uses a graft to widen a narrowed vessel. Bypass grafting reroutes blood around an obstruction, and extraanatomic bypass involves non-standard pathways to restore circulation. Thrombectomy focuses on removing blood clots obstructing vessels. The market also includes other innovative techniques and products, reflecting advancements in vascular repair and restoration.</p></p>
