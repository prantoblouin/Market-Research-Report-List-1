<p><ul><li>Horizontal</li><li>Vertical</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The bulb cartoning machine market is categorized into horizontal and vertical types based on the orientation of the carton formation process. Horizontal machines operate with cartons moving horizontally, offering efficient loading and high-speed production, ideal for high-volume applications. In contrast, vertical machines utilize a vertical flow for carton assembly, allowing for compact designs and ease of integration into existing lines, suitable for diverse product sizes. Both types serve varying operational needs in the packaging industry.</p></p>
