<p><p>The FTIR Exhaust Gas Analyzer market comprises two primary types: dilute measurement type and direct measurement type. The dilute measurement type analyzes exhaust gases by mixing them with a known amount of air, allowing for sensitive detection of pollutants in low concentrations. In contrast, the direct measurement type evaluates exhaust gases directly from the source, providing real-time data without dilution. Each method has its applications in environmental monitoring, regulatory compliance, and industrial processes, catering to different measurement requirements.</p></p>
