<p><p>The nephroureteral stent market is categorized into several types, each designed for specific clinical needs. Double J tubes feature a dual curve design to prevent migration and allow for optimal drainage. Multi loop J tubes offer additional flexibility and increased surface area, improving flow and reducing obstruction. These innovations cater to varying patient requirements in renal and urinary tract management.</p></p>
