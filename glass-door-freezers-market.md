<p><p>Glass door freezers are categorized into two main market types: hinged door and sliding door models. Hinged door freezers feature doors that swing open, providing easy access but requiring more space for opening. In contrast, sliding door freezers have horizontal doors that glide open, saving space and enabling quick access, making them ideal for high-traffic retail environments. Both types are popular in grocery stores, convenience shops, and restaurants for showcasing frozen products while maintaining visibility and aesthetic appeal.</p></p>
