Myoglobin test kits analyze elevated myoglobin levels, indicating muscle injury. CK-MB test kits measure creatine kinase enzyme levels, helping assess heart attacks. BNP test kits assess heart failure by measuring B-type natriuretic peptide levels. Other test kits include various markers and assays that support comprehensive cardiac diagnosis, enhancing clinical decision-making and patient management.</p></p>
