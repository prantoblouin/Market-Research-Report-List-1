<p><p>The nanoclays market is primarily segmented into three types: kaolinite, smectite, and other clays. Kaolinite, known for its high purity and chemical stability, is widely used in ceramics and plastics. Smectite, with its layered structure, offers exceptional swelling properties and is commonly utilized in drilling fluids and environmental applications. Other nanoclays encompass a variety of less common types that serve specialized applications in areas like agriculture, packaging, and pharmaceuticals, contributing to the diverse functionality of nanoclays in various industries.</p></p>
