<p><p>The automotive dashboard camera market is categorized into single-channel and multi-channel systems. Single-channel cameras capture video from a single perspective, typically focusing on the road ahead, making them ideal for basic security and accident documentation. Multi-channel systems, on the other hand, use multiple cameras to provide a comprehensive view, capturing footage from various angles, including the rear and sides of the vehicle. This enhances safety features, offering additional coverage for incidents and improving overall situational awareness for drivers.</p></p>
