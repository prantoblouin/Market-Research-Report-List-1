<p><p>The flexible printed electronics market encompasses various printing technologies, including inkjet, gravure, screen, flexography, and others. Inkjet printing offers precision and versatility for intricate designs, while gravure allows for high-speed, high-quality production, especially for large volumes. Screen printing is favored for its robustness and ability to handle thick inks, making it ideal for tactile applications. Flexography is known for its efficiency in high-volume printing with a wide range of substrates. Each method serves distinct applications and market needs, driving innovation in flexible electronics.</p></p>
