<p><p>The LED stair lighting market is primarily segmented into two types: LED step lights and LED strip lights. LED step lights are designed specifically for installation directly on stair risers or treads, providing focused illumination to enhance safety and visibility. In contrast, LED strip lights are flexible and can be installed along the edges of stairs, offering a continuous line of light for both aesthetic appeal and safety. Together, these products contribute to improved nighttime navigation and ambiance in residential and commercial spaces.</p></p>
