<p><p>The liquid analytical instrument market comprises various specialized analyzers designed for specific measurements in liquid samples. pH/ORP analyzers gauge acidity or alkalinity and oxidation-reduction potential, crucial for water quality. Ammonium analyzers detect nitrogen levels in water, while chlorine analyzers ensure safety in water treatment. Near infrared analyzers are used for detecting chemical compositions, enhancing process efficiency and quality control.</p></p>
