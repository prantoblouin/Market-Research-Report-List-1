<p><p>The Digital MEMS Microphone market is segmented into three key interface types: I2S, TDM, and PDM. I2S (Inter-IC Sound) is used for transmitting digital audio data between devices, offering low latency and high quality. TDM (Time Division Multiplexing) allows multiple microphones to share a single data line, optimizing space and improving system efficiency. PDM (Pulse Density Modulation) encodes audio signals more efficiently, making it ideal for low-power applications. Each interface caters to different use cases in audio technology.</p></p>
