<p><p>The ROV/AUV video camera market is primarily segmented into two types based on sensor technology: CMOS (Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor) and CCD (Charge-Coupled Device). CMOS sensors offer advantages such as lower power consumption, faster frame rates, and integrated processing capabilities, making them suitable for dynamic underwater environments. In contrast, CCD sensors are known for their superior image quality and low noise levels, making them ideal for detailed imaging in low-light conditions. Both types cater to various needs in marine exploration and monitoring.</p></p>
