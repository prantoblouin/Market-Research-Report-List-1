<p><p>The magnetic needle detector market is categorized into two main types: scroll and non-rolling. Scroll detectors feature a rotating mechanism that enhances sensitivity and allows for more precise detection of magnetic anomalies in various environments. Non-rolling detectors, on the other hand, typically have a stationary design, making them simpler to operate and easier to use in smaller or confined spaces. Both types serve essential roles in applications such as archaeological surveys, security, and industrial inspections, each offering distinct advantages depending on user needs.</p></p>
