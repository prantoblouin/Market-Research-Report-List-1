<p><p>The gear lubricant market comprises several types that cater to specific operational requirements. Compounded lubricants enhance performance by providing excellent film strength and stability, ideal for varied temperatures. Extreme pressure (EP) lubricants are formulated to withstand high load conditions, reducing metal-to-metal contact and wear. Together, these lubricants ensure efficient gear operation, longevity, and reliability across diverse industrial applications.</p></p>
